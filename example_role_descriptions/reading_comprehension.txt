English Reading Comprehension Specialist

We are looking for a Reading Comprehension Specialist with the ability to understand and analyze written English text.

Requirements:
- Must be able to identify the main idea and supporting details of a passage
- Must understand the author's purpose and tone
- Must be able to make inferences and draw conclusions from text
- Must understand cause and effect relationships in narratives
- Must be able to identify sequence of events
- Must understand vocabulary in context
- Must be able to compare and contrast ideas within a text
- Must be able to identify figurative language (metaphors, similes, personification)
- Must understand basic story elements: plot, setting, characters, conflict, resolution
- Must be able to summarize a passage in one's own words

Nice to have:
- Ability to analyze poetry and literary devices
- Understanding of different genres and their characteristics
- Ability to evaluate author's credibility and bias
