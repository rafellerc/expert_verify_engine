role_type: student
name: Basic Science

We are looking for a student with foundational knowledge in basic science.

Requirements:
- Must know the three states of matter (solid, liquid, gas)
- Must know basic chemistry: elements, compounds, mixtures
- Must know the periodic table first 10 elements (Hydrogen through Neon)
- Must understand Newton's three laws of motion
- Must know forms of energy: kinetic, potential, thermal
- Must understand the water cycle (evaporation, condensation, precipitation)
- Must know basic biology: cell structure, photosynthesis
