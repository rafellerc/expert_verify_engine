Basic Science Specialist

We are looking for a Basic Science Specialist with foundational knowledge in scientific concepts.

Requirements:
- Must understand the scientific method and experimental design
- Must know the three states of matter (solid, liquid, gas) and their properties
- Must understand basic chemistry: elements, compounds, mixtures, atomic structure
- Must know the periodic table first 20 elements (Hydrogen through Calcium)
- Must understand Newton's three laws of motion
- Must know forms of energy: kinetic, potential, thermal, chemical, electrical
- Must understand the water cycle (evaporation, condensation, precipitation, collection)
- Must know the structure of the Earth (crust, mantle, core) and plate tectonics
- Must understand basic biology: cell structure, photosynthesis, cellular respiration

Nice to have:
- Knowledge of the solar system and planetary order
- Understanding of the electromagnetic spectrum
- Knowledge of basic geological eras and fossil formation
