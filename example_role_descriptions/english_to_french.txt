English to French Translator

We are looking for an English to French Translator with basic language conversion skills.

Requirements:
- Must be able to translate common English phrases to French
- Must know basic French vocabulary (at least 100 common words)
- Must understand French sentence structure (subject-verb-object)
- Must know the present tense conjugations of common verbs (avoir, être, aller, faire)
- Must understand gender in French (masculine/feminine nouns)
- Must know numbers 1-20 in French
- Must know days of the week and months in French
- Must be able to form basic questions in French
- Must understand the difference between formal and informal "you" (vous vs tu)

Nice to have:
- Knowledge of French past tense (passé composé)
- Understanding of French negation
- Basic knowledge of French idioms
