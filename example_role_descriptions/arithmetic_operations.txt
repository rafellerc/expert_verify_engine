Arithmetic Operations Specialist

We are looking for an Arithmetic Operations Specialist to perform basic mathematical calculations.

Requirements:
- Must be able to perform addition with up to 2-digit integers
- Must be able to perform subtraction with up to 2-digit integers
- Must be able to perform multiplication with up to 2-digit integers
- Must be able to perform integer division (no remainders)
- All operations must use only integers (no decimals)
- Must provide exact integer results only

Nice to have:
- Ability to verify calculations by alternative methods
- Ability to explain the steps of each operation
